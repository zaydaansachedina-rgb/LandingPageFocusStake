FAQ

Got Questions?

Are the coins real money?

No! FocusStake uses virtual coins as a gamification system. You start with 100 free coins and earn more by completing sessions successfully. It's all about motivation, not money.

What happens if I fail a check-in?

Each session allows 3 strikes. If you miss a check-in or respond "No", you get a strike. 3 strikes = session fails and you lose your staked coins. Tough love that works.

How does AI Snap Solve work?

Take a photo of any problem (math, science, code, etc.) using your camera or gallery. Our GPT-4o integration analyzes the image and returns a detailed step-by-step solution in seconds.

What study methods are available?

Choose Pomodoro (25 min work / 5 min break), Deep Focus (50 min work / 10 min break), or Custom timer (15 to 120 minutes). You can also enable ambient sounds.

What subjects does it support?

All of them! During onboarding, pick from Math, Science, Languages, Programming, History, Literature, Business, Art & Design, Music, and Test Prep.

Is FocusStake free?

Yes! The free tier includes coin staking, timers, check-ins, streaks, badges, leveling, and basic analytics. Premium adds unlimited AI features, ambient sounds, and advanced analytics.

What platforms does it support?

FocusStake is built with React Native and launches on both iOS and Android on March 1, 2026. Sign in with email/password or Google.

Can I use it offline?

Timer and coin staking work offline. AI features (Snap Solve, Quiz Generation) require an internet connection since they use GPT-4o. Your data syncs when you're back online.
